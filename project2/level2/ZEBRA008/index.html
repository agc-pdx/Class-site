PUT ZEBRAS BY THE MISSISSIPPIELI SIEGEL


Swiftly in forests, the zebra,
                                                             Slowly the Mississippi;

Zebras are by the Mississippi,

&Not so by, but by.
Where the palm-tree waves slowly in heat,
with many-colored, little live things all about,

  • green,
    • orange,
      • pink,
    • black,
  • purple,
    • red,
      • and all,


            • O, do say
              • the Mississippi
                • flows slowly.
              • Hell, is not
            • the same moon
              • over zebras and
                • the Mississippi?

The moon’s over all.
The moon’s not so big.
Zebras are by the Mississippi.

Purple’s by the Mississippi.
Palm-trees are.
Anywhere’s anywhere,

anywhere’s everywhere.
Anything’s everywhere.
Put zebras by the Mississippi; O, do. O, do.
