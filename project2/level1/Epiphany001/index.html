MARK PETERSEN
I Got Drunk Then Poetry
_____________________

I am my  a  r  t.

Its imperfections are my own

   exposure.

Old work is my biography

   and to criticize it unfairly is not loving myself.

Rushing and half-effort becomes personal insult.

I want to give you the best parts of me

   who I really believe I am.

If I produce great art it is because I am my greatest fan.

How could you ever believe in me if I didn’t first?
