Epiphany002

MARK PETERSEN
I Got Drunk Then Poetry
_____________________

I am my art. Its imperfections are my own exposure. Old work is my biography and to criticize it unfairly is not loving myself. Rushing and half-effort becomes personal insult. I want to give you the best parts of me who I really believe I am. If I produce great art it is because I am my greatest fan. How could you ever believe in me if I didn’t first? It would be sad not to love your own art.       A guitar is a tool of art sometimes I think I’d like to have one but in my hands it could do nothing. I do have a typewriter a different kind of paintbrush for a different kind of art Everyone has an instrument.
